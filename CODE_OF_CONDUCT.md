# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in this project, and its community, will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to creating a positive environment include:

- **Be respectful**: Treat all contributors with courtesy and respect
- **Be inclusive**: Welcome people of all backgrounds and perspectives
- **Be constructive**: Provide helpful feedback and suggestions
- **Be patient**: Not everyone has the same experience or knowledge level
- **Listen actively**: Try to understand different viewpoints
- **Assume good intent**: Give people the benefit of the doubt
- **Collaborate**: Work together toward shared goals
- **Be professional**: Keep discussions on-topic and focused

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation
- Discrimination based on race, gender, identity, or belief
- Offensive comments or jokes
- Disruptive behavior in discussions
- Personal attacks or insults
- Exclusionary language or actions
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Any form of abuse

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (GitHub, discussions, issues, pull requests)
- Official project events
- Any spaces representing the project publicly

## Enforcement

Violations of this code can be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be:
- Reviewed promptly
- Investigated fairly
- Handled confidentially

Consequences for violations may include:
- Warning and discussion
- Content removal
- Temporary or permanent ban from community participation
